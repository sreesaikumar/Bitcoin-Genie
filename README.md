<h1>Bitcoin Genie</h1>
Welcome to Bitcoin Genie! You can find complete information about bitcoin i.e what is the current price, what is the underlying technology behind it, how does it work, price of bitcoin in the history and even you can also know how much you would have earned if you have invested in bitcoin on any specific period in the past.

<h2>Get Started</h2>

Enable Bitcoin Genie action from google's actions store.

**Try Saying,** 

*Hey Google, "Talk to Bitcoin Genie."*

- *Would I be rich if I bought bitcoins?*
- *What is a bitcoin?*
- *What is the latest price of bitcoin?"*
- *How much I would have earned if I bought bitcoins for 3,50,000 rupees a year ago?"*
- *What is the technology behind bitcoin currency?*
- *Tell me the bitcoin pricing history in dollars*
